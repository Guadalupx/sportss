It is essential that an athlete's diet includes proteins, fats, carbohydrates, and fiber. Also, to perform at their best in training and competitions, it is very important to consume quality foods. If the athlete is vegetarian it is important to take into account plant based food, these only count as a protein source if you do not consume the other protein sources in the protein category.

DRINK ADVERTENCE:

Sport drinks like Powerade or Gatorade are no better for you than water to keep you hydrated during sports. But if you exercise for more than 60 to 90 minutes or in very hot weather, sports drinks may be a good option. The extra carbs and electrolytes may improve performance in these conditions. Otherwise your body will do just as well with water.
